Getting to the Wedding Destination

For those travelling from abroad and will be flying in, we recommend that you fly to Copenhagen Airport. From Copenhagen Airport there is a train that goes directly from the airport to Helsingborg Central Station.

If you are staying in Viken:

From Helsingborg Central Station you can either get a taxi or the bus 220 that goes directly to Viken.

If you are staying in Höganäs:

From Helsingborg Central Station you can either get a taxi or the bus 220 or SkåneExpressen 11 which goes directly to Höganäs.

If you are staying in Jonstorp:

From Helsingborg Central Station you can either get a taxi or the bus 220 that goes to Höganäs and then change to bus 225 (this is sometimes the same bus so check with the bus driver before getting off).

If you are staying at Bläsinge Gård:

If you staying at Bläsinge Gård we recommend you to either take a taxi or rent a car. You can take the bus to Jonstorp but there will then be a 20 min walk along country roads.

Accommodation

We recommend that you stay in either Viken or around Bläsinge Gård, however please note that all the small towns in Kullabygden are fairly close so staying there will not be a problem if you prefer this. There are several Airbnb, rental cottages and minor hotels in the area. If you do have any questions regarding places to stay then please don't hesitate to get in touch with us.

Taxi

Uber: You can use Uber in the area but might take 15-20 min to get a car out in the country side at Bläsinge Gård.

Sverige Taxi: You can download the app Sverigetaxi or call them on 042 - 180 200.

Taxi Höganäs: 042-362164

Transportation to the Wedding Ceremony

For people staying around the area it is fairly easy to use public transport or order a taxi to take you to Viken. For those staying at Bläsinge Gård, we will arrange for a bus to transfer you to the venue.

Transport After the Party

You will have to make your own arrangements to get home. However please note that we will also have people on the day that will be able to assist with driving people back home, but if you want to be safe we recommend to book a taxi in advance!

Transportation to the Dinner

Transport will be arranged for all wedding guests after the wedding ceremony and drinks to the venue.
